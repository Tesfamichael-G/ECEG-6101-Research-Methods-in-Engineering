⚙️ Core Concepts: Research ↔ Design

Investigates the unknown

Creates new, generalizable knowledge. Follows the scientific method: question → hypothesis → experimentation → analysis → conclusion.

Example in CommsEng

“How does beamforming algorithm X impact latency in mmWave 5G under NLOS conditions?”

Solves a specific problem

Uses known methods to build a product or system. Goal: a working, optimized solution.

Example in CommsEng

“Design a low‑latency beamforming codebook for a commercial 5G base station.”

Aspect🔬 Research🛠️ Design
GoalDiscover/explainBuild/solve
OutcomePaper, thesis, theoryPrototype, product, system
ApproachHypothesis testing, variable controlIterative prototyping, trade‑offs

💡 Many M.Sc. projects blend both: research informs design, design validates research.
