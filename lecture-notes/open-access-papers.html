  2. Check availability in the Paper Selection Tracker before claiming.
  3. Download the PDF and read carefully—focus on methodology, not just results.
  4. Use the Literature Review Matrix template to analyze:
    • What problem does it solve?
    • What methodology/approach is used?
    • What are the key findings?
    • What limitations/gaps remain?
    • How is it relevant to YOUR proposed research?
  5. Write a 2-3 page critical review focusing on methodology critique (not just summary).
