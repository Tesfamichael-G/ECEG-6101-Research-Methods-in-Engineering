📡 2. Choose Right Database

✅ IEEE Xplore – Best for electrical engineering & communications
✅ Scopus – Multidisciplinary, citation tracking
📄 arXiv – Preprints, fast but not peer-reviewed
🌐 Google Scholar – Broad, but less reproducibility

🗂️ Zotero Workflow Checklist

Install Zotero Connector (browser extension)
Create folder by research topic
Import PDFs & extract metadata
Annotate and tag key claims
Use “Better BibTeX” for LaTeX export
Share group library with supervisor

📋 3. Systematic Review Workflow (PRISMA-like)

1 Formulate research question (PICO framework)
2 Select databases & search strategy – use Boolean strings
3 Screen titles/abstracts – inclusion/exclusion criteria
4 Full‑text retrieval & quality assessment (e.g., using CASP)
5 Data extraction & synthesis – create evidence table
6 Write review: thematic, chronological, or methodological
✅ Use JBI Critical Appraisal Checklist or Cochrane Risk of Bias for engineering intervention studies.
