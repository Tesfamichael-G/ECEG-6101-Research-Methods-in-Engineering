The "Gap" Concept

Your research must fill a hole in existing knowledge.

  • Evidence Gap: Contradictory findings in previous studies.
  • Knowledge Gap: A variable hasn't been studied yet.
  • Methodological Gap: Previous methods were flawed or outdated.

Search Strategies

Effective searching requires Boolean Logic.

Click to Expand: Boolean Operators
  • AND: Narrows results (e.g., RIS AND Beamforming)
  • OR: Broadens results (e.g., 5G OR Sixth Generation)
  • NOT: Excludes terms (e.g., Machine Learning NOT Deep Learning)
Click to Expand: Wildcards & Phrases
  • Quotes: "Massive MIMO" searches for exact phrase.
  • Asterisk: communicat* finds communicate, communication, communications.

Recommended Databases for Comm. Eng:

  1. IEEE Xplore: The gold standard for electrical engineering.
  2. Scopus / Web of Science: Multidisciplinary, high impact.
  3. arXiv.org: Pre-prints (fastest, but not peer-reviewed yet).
