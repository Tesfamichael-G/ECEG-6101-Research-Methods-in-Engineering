Research vs. Design

Design

  • Solves a specific problem
  • Uses known methods
  • Goal: A working product

Research

  • Investigates the unknown
  • Creates new knowledge
  • Goal: Generalizable truth

The Research Question

A good RQ must be Specific and Testable.


// Bad: "Study 5G Networks"
// Good: "How does Beamforming Algorithm X
